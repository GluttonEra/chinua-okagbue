Process

Evaluation workflow

The consistent methodology I apply to every review engagement.

01
Review Guidelines

Study the task rubric, scoring criteria, and domain-specific guidelines thoroughly before beginning any review.

02
Analyze Outputs

Fully read or inspect the submitted output — code, prose, image, or UI — without premature scoring decisions.

03
Identify Errors

Catalog factual, structural, and quality issues with precise references, categorized by type and severity.

04
Document Findings

Write structured rationale for each issue — precise enough to withstand independent review.

05
Deliver Structured Assessment

Submit calibrated score with documented evidence, formatted to platform or client specification.
